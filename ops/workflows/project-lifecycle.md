# ESW Project Lifecycle

## Phase 0: Intake & Qualification

**Owner**: Project Controller
**Duration**: 1-3 days

| Step | Action | Output |
|---|---|---|
| 0.1 | Client fills intake form or ESW captures brief | Completed Intake Form |
| 0.2 | Project Controller classifies project | Classification: Standard EIA / BNG+Credits / Full Regen / Advisory |
| 0.3 | Initial fee estimate and timeline | Engagement Proposal |
| 0.4 | Client sign-off | Signed Scope of Work |

**Gate**: Client approval to proceed. Master File created.

---

## Phase 1: Discovery & Baseline

**Owner**: Eco-Scientist + GIS Analyst
**Duration**: 2-6 weeks (depending on site access and data availability)

| Step | Action | Owner | Output |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1.1 | Desktop study: existing EIA, protected area maps, species records | Eco-Scientist | Desktop Review Report |
| 1.2 | Spatial analysis: site mapping, land use, constraints overlay | GIS Analyst | Constraint Maps (GIS layers) |
| 1.3 | Field survey (if required): habitat assessment, species inventory | Eco-Scientist | Field Survey Report |
| 1.4 | Regulatory scan: applicable national/international regulations, permits needed | Legal Compliance | Regulatory Memo |
| 1.5 | Compile baseline package | Eco-Scientist | Ecological Baseline Report |

**Gate**: Baseline approved by Project Controller. Risks identified and classified.

---

## Phase 2: Strategy Design

**Owner**: Regen-Architect
**Duration**: 2-4 weeks

| Step | Action | Owner | Output |
|---|---|---|---|
| 2.1 | Apply mitigation hierarchy to identified risks | Regen-Architect | Mitigation Hierarchy Matrix |
| 2.2 | Design Nature-based Solutions (NbS) for site | Regen-Architect | NbS Concept Note |
| 2.3 | Landscape integration plan | Regen-Architect + GIS Analyst | Design Drawings / Spatial Plans |
| 2.4 | Define monitoring KPIs | Eco-Scientist + Regen-Architect | Monitoring Framework |
| 2.5 | Technical review | Eco-Scientist | Peer Review Sign-off |

**Gate**: Strategy approved by client. Ready for financial structuring.

---

## Phase 3: Financial Structuring

**Owner**: Green Financier
**Duration**: 2-4 weeks

| Step | Action | Owner | Output |
|---|---|---|---|
| 3.1 | Quantify ecosystem service values | Green Financier | Valuation Report |
| 3.2 | Model carbon credit potential (Verra/Gold Standard methodology) | Green Financier | Carbon Credit Forecast |
| 3.3 | Model biodiversity credit potential | Green Financier | BNG/Credit Assessment |
| 3.4 | Assess green bond / sustainability-linked loan eligibility | Green Financier | Bankability Memo |
| 3.5 | Build integrated financial model (CAPEX, OPEX, Revenue, IRR) | Green Financier | Financial Model (Excel) |
| 3.6 | Prepare investor-ready summary | Green Financier + Brand Voice | Investment Teaser |

**Gate**: Financial viability confirmed. Proceed to legal.

---

## Phase 4: Legal Review & Contracting

**Owner**: Legal Compliance
**Duration**: 1-3 weeks

| Step | Action | Owner | Output |
|---|---|---|---|
| 4.1 | Land tenure verification | Legal Compliance | Title / Tenure Memo |
| 4.2 | Permitting roadmap | Legal Compliance | Permitting Plan |
| 4.3 | Liability assessment | Legal Compliance | Risk Memo |
| 4.4 | Draft/review contracts (land access, credit sales, service agreements) | Legal Compliance | Contract Package |
| 4.5 | CSRD/TNFD compliance check | Legal Compliance + Eco-Scientist | Compliance Report |

**Gate**: Legal clearance. All contracts in place.

---

## Phase 5: Implementation Support

**Owner**: Project Controller (coordination) + Regen-Architect (technical)
**Duration**: Variable (months to years)

| Step | Action | Owner | Output |
|---|---|---|---|
| 5.1 | Contractor selection and briefing | Project Controller | Contractor Shortlist |
| 5.2 | Implementation supervision | Regen-Architect | Site Progress Reports |
| 5.3 | Stakeholder coordination | Project Controller | Meeting Minutes |
| 5.4 | Adaptive management (adjust design based on field conditions) | Regen-Architect | Revised Plans |

**Gate**: Implementation milestones met. Monitoring phase begins.

---

## Phase 6: Monitoring & Reporting

**Owner**: Eco-Scientist + Green Financier
**Duration**: Ongoing (annual cycles)

| Step | Action | Owner | Output |
|---|---|---|---|
| 6.1 | Ecological monitoring (species, habitats, soil, water) | Eco-Scientist | Annual Ecology Report |
| 6.2 | Credit verification and issuance support | Green Financier | Credit Verification Docs |
| 6.3 | Financial performance tracking | Green Financier | Annual Financial Report |
| 6.4 | TNFD/CSRD annual disclosure support | Legal Compliance + Eco-Scientist | Disclosure Report |
| 6.5 | Client reporting and board presentation | Project Controller | Annual Executive Brief |

**Gate**: Annual review completed. Continue, adjust, or close.
